The first 5 books of Wings Of Fire are set in a world at war. There is a prophecy known simply as the dragonet prophecy.
"When the war has lasted twenty years...
the dragonets will come.
When the land is soaked in blood and tears...
the dragonets will come.
Find the Seawing egg of deepest blue.
Wings of night shall come to you.
The largest egg in mountain high
will give to you the wings of sky.
For wings of earth search through the mud
for an egg the color of dragon blood.
And hidden away from the tival queens,
the Sandwing egg awaits unseen.
Of three queens who blister and blaze and burn,
two shall die and one shall learn
if she bows to a fate that is stronger and higher,
she'll have the power of wings of fire.
Five eggs to hatch on brightest night,
five dragons born to end the fight.
Darkness will rise to bring the light.
The dragonets are coming."

The first book is The Dragonet Prophecy. It is told from the perspective of Clay, the Mudwing in the prophecy. The dragonets were kept inside a cave for the first 6 years of their life. They escape and then get captured by the evil Skywing queen. They escape and then head down the diamond spray river and find Clay's brothers and sisters.

The second book is The Lost Heir. It is told from the prophecy Seawing, Tsunami. The dragonets are at the sea when a SKywing patrol goes by and Tsunami attacks a soldier because she thought they saw the other dragonets. They then head into the Kingdom of the Sea and find Tsunami's mother, Queen Coral. A flood occurs along with a recents string of murders of the royal dragonets while they are still in their eggs.
