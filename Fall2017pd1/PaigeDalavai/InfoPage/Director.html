The director of Band Baaja Baaraat is Maneesh Sharma. He is very famous, and he has won a good amount of awards for example: Filmfare Award for Best Dubut Director, Nation film Award for Best Feature Film in Hindi and Guild Award for Best Dubut Director. Some other movies Maneesh Sharma has directed is Ladies vs. Ricky Bahl, Fan, Shuddh Desi and many others.
