SIGNIFICANT CHARACTERS
Uriel Septim: The second to last Emperor of Tamriel. Assassinated by the Mythic Dawn cult.
Martin Septim: The last Emperor of Tamriel and the final son of Uriel Septim.
Baurus: The youngest and most accomplished of the Blades. Also Martin Septim's bodygaurd.
Jauffre: Grandmaster of the Blades and the Order of Talos.
Mankar Camoran: Leader on the Mythic Dawn.
Akatosh: The Head of the Nine Divines. Protaganist.
Mehrunes Dagon: The Daedric Prince of Destruction. Antagonist.
You: The Champion of Cyrodill.
