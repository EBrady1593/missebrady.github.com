Cape May New Jersey

When you go to the beach, you can rent out the best hotel named Summer Station. When you go to Cape May the hotel, you can get a beach front view or you can get a non beachfront view, which is worse because then you can not see the ocean when you wake up. There are other hotels there but I think Summer Station is the best hotel for the beach.
