In Cape May you can go to the beach and the boardwalk and you can go in town. When you go to the beach you can surf fish and you can walk or swim or you can just relax on the beach and just watch the waves.At te boardwalk you can shop and buy things they have resturants there and taffy shops and candy shops. They have bluefish and flounder and sriped bass, whiting/Southern Kingfish.
