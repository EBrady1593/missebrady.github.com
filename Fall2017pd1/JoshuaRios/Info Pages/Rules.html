Rules

When you break a rule in basketbal, it's called a foul.

If you commit 6 fouls in a 48-minute game, you get ejected out of the game.

There are multiple types of fouls, such as a shooting foul, a techincal foul, or a personal foul.

A shooting foul is when the offenseive player goes up for a shot, but the defensive player hits his foreare, or any where else

A techinacal foul is when a defensive player does completely unecessary to the offenseive player, and most of the time, the player on defense gets ejected.

Amd finally, a personal foul is when a player commits one of the fouls above, and gets fined from a range of $5,000 to anywhere above that, depending on how bad the foul was.
