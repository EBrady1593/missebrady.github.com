NATSU DRAGNEEL

Natsu Dragneel is one of the primary male protagonist of the series. He is Zeref's younger brother, Natsu was resurrected as one of his brother's over 400 years old as an Etherious, which is known as "Etherious Natsu Dragneel" or E.N.D for short. At some point durning Natsu's youth, Zeref turns him over to Igneel, who trains Natsu as a dragon slayer, a wizard with dragon-like phsiology whose magic is designed to combat other dragon's. Natsu is immune to flames and is also capable of consuming flames to fuel his Fire Dragon Slayer Magic attacks, which include breathing fire and igniting any part of his boady of use when striking. For example, His hands and/or his feet. However, he suffers from severe motion sickness when he is in a vehicle of any sort. He eventually learns to use Dragon Force, an advance form of dragon slayer magic that boosts his strength and speed, and covers parts of his body in scales. After witnessing Igneel's disappearance, Natsu embarks on a journey to find Igneel. His search leads him to join Fairy Tail at a young age, gaining a title as "Salamander" one of the guild's most loyal yet reckless members who causes most of the collateral damage for which his giuild os blamed.

Ur took Gray as her second student and taught Gray and Lyon Ice Make magic. Gray accepted this training because he wanted to get his revenge on what Deliora did by using Ur's unorthodox methods. After a few days of training, Gray heard about Deliora's current location and he decied to challenge it but he ignored Ur and Lyon's warnings and did this anyways. Gray then collapsed in exaustion and woke up to see Ur battleing Deliora. Ur told Gray to take Lyon and run far away from Deliora and Ur. Gray carried Lyon and saw that Ur lost her leg but she replaced it with ice. Suddenly Lyon wakes up as started to cast a certain spell called the Ice Shell but Ur stopped Lyon by freezing him. Ur explained to Gray that the Ice Shell was the only way to defeat Deliora and she was surpried to know that Lyon had tried to do what she had explained. Ur then used the Ice Shell spell which caused her body to be destroyed. But before she turned into ice she made Gray promise to tell Lyon that she was dead so then Lyon wouldn't waste his whole life trying to undo the Ice Shell spell and she also told him to step into the future, since she would be sealing his darkness. The nexy day, Lyon woke up to discover that Deliora was encased in ice and was Ur's sacrifice. Lyon then blamed Gray for Ur's deat and the two of them parted ways.

LUCY HEARTFILIA

Lucy Heartfilia is the primary female protagonist of the series. She is a 17- year old celestial wizard who uses a set of magical Gatekeys to summon celestail spirits. She can also fight with a whip, which she upgrades to the extendable energy whip Fleuve d'Etoiles (French for "River of Stars"). At the age of 16, Lucy flees from her home to pirsue her dream of joinng Fairy Tail. Her efforts lead to conflict with a slave trader named Bora, and Natsu and Happy rescue her in exchange for her joining Fairy Tail. She performs guild-posted jobs with Natsu and Happy to fund her rented house in Magnolia, and is bewildered by her guildmates' antics. She is also an aspiring novelist, and writes letters about her adventures to Layla Heartfilia, her deceased mother. Eventually, Lucy possesses 10 of the 12 golden keys that allow her to summon the rare and powerful Zodiac spirits and gain the ability to do magical Star Dresses that allow her direct access to her zodiac spirits' powers.

HAPPY

Happy is a 6-year old anthropomorphic cat with blue fur who can also talk. He is Natsu's best friend and sole travling companion at the start of the series. Happy possesses a magic ability called Aera (wings) which allows him to grow white feathered wings and fly at great speeds, giving him the appearance of a winged cat. He uses this form to carry Natsu in the air, and is the only one Natsu is able to ride without succumbing to motion sickness. He was hatched from an egg found by Natsu and Lisanna Strauss, who named him "Happy" after his birth cheers up their guildmates' in the middle of a disagreement.

ERZA SCARLET

Erza Scarlet is one of the primary female protagonist in the series and in Team Natsu. Erza is a 19 year-old swordswoman and is the daughter of the Dragon Queen, Irene Belsrion. At the age of 11, Ezra was captured by followers of zeref and was a slave girl at the Tower of Heaven. She befriends some of the other slaves including Jellal. Erza then rallies the other slaves for a mass break out to rescue Jellal from being tortured and losing an eye in the process. However, Jellal was brainwashed into serving Zeref and turns on her so then he can finish the construction on the tower. Having gained her requip magic, she can use "The Knight" magic which allows her to summon various weapons and armors that enhance her abilities at will. She also gains an artificial eye that grants her immunity to illusions and optical magic. Eventually Erza becomes Fairy Tail's disciplinarian as well as one of he guild's S-class wizards, gainig the infamy as "Titania" which means "Queen of the Fairies". Hpwever, Erza grows fond of her guildmates after Natsu defeated Jellal and rescued her. Erza later helps Jellal atone for his misdeeds after he is restored to his old self, which inspires him to organize Crime Sociere seven years later. One year later after Makarov's disappearence, Erza temporaraily replaces him as the guilds seventh master.

GAJEEL REDFOX

Gajeel Redfox is an Iron Dragon Slayer, this type of magic allows him to change certain parts of his body into iron weapons suck as clubs and swords the cover his body with nearly indestructable steel scales, and breathe gusts of wind containig metal shards capable of shredding a person's body. He can also consume metal objects for nurishmment. Adopted and raised by the dragon Metalicana, who disappears around the same time as Igneel did. Gajeel joins the hantom Lord guild and eventually gains the infamy as "Kurogane" which means "Black Steel". His attack on Levy McGarden's team and the destruction of the Fairy Tail's guildhall results in war between the two guilds. After the Phantom Lord guild is defeated, Gajeel joins Fairy Tail and eventually deveolps and strong loyalty to the guild. He also comes to be on good terms with Levy after defending her from Laxus before the Battle of Fairy Tail, and later he partners with her for the S-class wizard test on Sirius Island. Following the guilds war with Tartaros, Gajeel becomes a captain of the Magic Council arrest and custody division with Panther Lily and Levy; he retires from the sevice when Fairy Tail is reorganized.
