Early Life

Jaquelin was born in Oxford, England and grew up there.
Her mother, Iris Greep, was a talented pianist and
taught at the Royal Academy of Music.
Later in her life, she moved all throughout Europe.
She took part in many performances
and festivals when she was young.
At age four, she had heard the sound of the cello
and asked her mother for one,
so her mother bagan to teach her.
