This is the Nancy Drew and the Hardy Boys fan page. The links connect to information about Nancy Drew, the Hardy Boys, and the Hardy Boys/Nancy Drew Mysteries show. Both series of these books were written in the 1930's. The writer of the original Nancy Drew books wrote under the pseudonym Carolyn Keene, and the writer of the original Hardy Boys series wrote under the pseudonym Franklin W. Dixon.


Nancy Drew, the intelligent girl detective, and The Hardy Boys, the witty detective brothers, are still popular with fans everywhere.
