The New York Yankees are a baseball team that play in the Mlb. The Yankees play in the Bronx in Yankee Stadium. The Yankees are the most coveted sports team ever because they have won 27 world series, the most by any team and 16 more than any other team. They also have been to 40 world series, 20 more than any other team.
