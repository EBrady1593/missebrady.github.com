The Three Movies

The Lord of the Rings The Fellowship of the Ring

The Lord of the Rings The Fellowship of the Ring is the first Lord of the Rings movie in the series. The first movie was released on December 19, 2001 and the movie took place in New Zealand.

The Lord of the Rings The Two Towers

The Lord of the Rings The Two Towers is the second movie in the series. This movie was released on December 18, 2002. The budge of the second movie was 94 million US dollars.

The Lord of The Rings The Return of The King

The Lord of The Rings The Return of The King is the third movie and the last movie in The Lord of The Rings series. This movie was released on December 17, 2003. The budge for this movie was also 94 million US dollars.
