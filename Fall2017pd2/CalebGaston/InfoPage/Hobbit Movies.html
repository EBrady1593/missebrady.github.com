These are all three Hobbit Movies

The Hobbit An Unexpected Journay

The Hobbit An Unexpected Journey was the first Hobbit movies in the series. This movie was released on December 12, 2012. This film grossed over $1 billion in the Box Office.

The Hobbit The Desolation of Smaug

The Hobbit The Desolation of Smaug is the second Hobbbit movie in the series. This movie was released on December 11, 2013. This film grossed over $958 million in the Box Office.

The Hobbit The Battle of The Five Armies

The Hobbit The Battle of The Five Armies is the last movie of the Hobbit series. This movie was released on December 11, 2014. This film grossed over $956 million worldwide.
