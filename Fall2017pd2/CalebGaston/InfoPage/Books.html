These are the three books of The Lord of The Rings

The Lord of The Rings The Fellowship of The Ring

The Lord of The Rings The Fellowship of The Ring is the first book in the series of the books. This book was published on July 29, 1954 by Allen and Unwin. The author of this book is J.R.R Tolkien.

The Lord of The Rings The Two Towers

The Lord of The Rings The Two Towers is the second book of the series. This book was published on November 11, 1954 by Allen and Unwin. The author of this book is J.R.R Tolkien.

The Lord of The Rings The Return of The King

The Lord of The Rings The Return of The King is the last book in the series. This book was published on October 20, 1955 by Allen and Uuwin. The author of this book is J.R.R Tolkien.
