A damage type is an attribute of a weapon or ability that has potential to do bonus damage depending on an enemy's shield or whether or not a Burn modifier is active. ... In Destiny, burn modifiers increase the damage of weapons and abilities, depending on their damage types, up to 200% for Guardians and 300% for enemies.

Kinetic - This is white and is the default for the majority of weapons in Destiny 2. It has no bonus damage to any enemy. Solar - This is orange and causes Solar damage. It causes more damage to Solar enemies who have an orange shield. ... It causes more damage to Arc enemies who have a blue shield.
