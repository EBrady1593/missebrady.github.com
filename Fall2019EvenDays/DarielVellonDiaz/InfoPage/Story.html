Curse of Osiris is a downloadable content (DLC) expansion pack for Destiny 2. ... The events of Curse of Osiris take place on Mercury and follow the Guardian's efforts to rescue Osiris from the Vex in order to avert a dark future. The expansion's plot touches on the relationship between Osiris and his disciple, Ikora Rey.
