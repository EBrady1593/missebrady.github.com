Workouts

For a good boxing career you must maintain a good workout routine. Cardio, lots of cardio is a very very important factor in Boxing. It all depends on the person and its not just how you workout but also how you eat. (NOT WHAT YOU EAT!!!!!) So i'll just give my routine.

Running

tredmill- 10 to 20 minutes at 5.5 or 6. Laps- 15 laps, 5 full speed running and 10 jogging.

Sit-ups

5 sets- 20 to 30 sit-ups each

Jumping Jacks

10 sets of 30

Squats

3 or 4 sets of 20 or 30

Lunges

2 or 3 sets of 10 or 15

Jumping Rope

20 reps doing about 5 sets keeping it constant.

Pull-ups

2 sets of 5 or 10 until you get enough muscle and strength to do more then you double it each time.
