Scheduling

Scheduling consists of the rounds, the times and the seconds down.

Rounds

The rounds depend on two things. Depending on the age group it ranges from nine to twelve rounds. It also depends on the win. if someone wins in just a few rounds then of course you would end it there.

Minutes Per Round

There are usaually three minutes per round and they feel like the longest three minutes of your life. it seems short but when you are in the ring it feels like forever.

Seconds Down

If you go down the ref usaually starts counting. if you get up before ten seconds then you are still in. if you are down or get thr towel thrown in by your coach, then the other person is the champion and you lost that entire match. it does not matter if you won mutiple rounds.
