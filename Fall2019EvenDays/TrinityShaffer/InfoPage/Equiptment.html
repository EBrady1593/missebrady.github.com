Equiptment

There is a series of different equiptment that is needed for the boxing process.

Boxing Gloves

Boxing gloves are usaually red or black, you can choose a variety of colors but in that case it would probably be your own customized gloves. They usaually come in an average of 3 sizes. Large,Medium, and small. 3 sizes that fit all. usaually you want something a little bigger than you hand size alone because of the padding underneath.
Boxing Gloves

Wraps

Until you get into Profetional Boxing, you use things called wraps. In pro boxing, you would usaually use tape fro under your gloves. The pourpose of wraps is to not have as big of a chance of breaking you knuckles. It also gives you more padding so when you make a harder hit you dont do more damage to your hands, it also keeps your writsts from cracking by going all over the place.
Wraps

Speed Bag

A speed bag is a great practice and workout for boxing. You can gain speed in your hitting and the objective is to keep it consistant. It looks easy but for a starter, getting it going at a constant rate is actually pretty hard. You can go to a gym (for boxing) to use a speed bag or you can have your own. Usaually the equiptment is not too expensive but it can be.
Speed Bag

Heavy Bag

A heavy bag is the most commonly known for boxing practice. Dont worry about sizes for this one because one size works for all body types and shapes and heights. A heavy bag can withstand a lot more powered hits which is a difference betweem the speed bag and the heavy bag.
Heavy Bag

Weights

Weights are a good way to prepare for having a stronger and tougher build. Leg weights, Arm weights, and belive it or not, head weights.
