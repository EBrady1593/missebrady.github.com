Jordan 3

This is the information for Jordan 3s

Air Jordan 3 "Black/Cement"

The Air Jordan 3 was the first of many for Michael Jordan and Tinker Hatfield. It was the first time they would work together on the signature shoe, it was the first time the elephant print made an appearance on a shoe, and it was the first time the “Jumpman” logo adorned the tongue with the classic “NIKE AIR” stamped on the heel.

Air Jordan 3 "White/Cement"

The Air Jordan 3 White/Cement was an original color from 1988. It features a white tumbled leather upper with Fire Red details on the lower eyelets, tongue lining, and Jumpman logo. The sneaker also sports the trademark elephant print on the mudguard and heel, as well as a partially encapsulated Air unit in the midsole for responsive cushioning.

Air Jordan 3 "Fire Red"

Originally released in 1988, the Air Jordan 3 OG ‘Fire Red’ features a white leather upper with Fire Red accents on the eyelets, collar, and midsole. The sneaker also boasts the iconic elephant print on the toe cap and heel and a partially encapsulated Air unit in the midsole.
