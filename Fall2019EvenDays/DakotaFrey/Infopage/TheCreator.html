The Creator

Alex Hirsch is the creative director, voice actor, and animator for "Gravity Falls." He was born on June 18, 1985 in Peidmont, Calafornia. Sound familier? Alex took things from his own life and put it into this show like twins. That's right Alex has a twin sister, Ariel Hirsch. Most of the caricteristics in Mabel are from her.

Alex is mostly known for "Gravity Falls" but he's also been apart of many other shows as well. Some including "Rick and Morty" and "Fish Hooks."

Sadly, "Gravity Falls" has been over for about three years but we're all waiting to see what Alex has in store for us. Alex been working on something new for Netflix and he's keeping it secret and hidden for awhile now.

Fun Facts

  • Soos if his favorite characters to voice.

  • He earned and has been nominated for BAFTA and Annie awards.

  • In 2017 Alex, Justin Rolland and Ethan klein raised more than $200,000 on a live-stream for hurricane Harvey.

  • His favorite show growing up was "The Simpsons."

  • His favorite character from "Gravity Falls" is Bill Cipher.

  • Dipper and his adventures are based on what Alex wanted to do in his boring summers as a kid.
