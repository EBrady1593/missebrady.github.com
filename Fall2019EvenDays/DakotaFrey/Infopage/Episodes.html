"The Time Traveler's Pig"

Mabel wins her new pet pig, Waddles, at the Mystery Fair, while Dipper has to watch Wendy and Robbie go out. Once the fair ends, the twins meet time traveler Blendin. Dipper steals his time machine to fix his mistake so Wendy and Robbie won't end up together. Mabel decides to tag along so she can relive the moment she won Waddles.

"Little Dipper"

Gideon wants to take the Mystery Shack in order to get something. While Dipper get's jealous that Mabel is taller than him. He decides to find a crystal that will make him taller. Once Mabel finds out about the crystal, her and Dipper fight over who gets to be taller. Gideon comes and shrinks the twins with the crystal and is going to use it on Stan to get the Shack.

"Summerween"

It's Summerween in Gravity Falls, and Dipper and Mabel plan on going trick or treating together. At least until Dipper gets invited to a Halloween party with Wendy. As Dipper was about to tell Mabel he couldn't trick or treat that night, the Summerween Trickster came to their door. He threatened them saying if they didn't get 500 peices of candy that night they would die. Dipper's now forced to go trick or treating with Mabel and her friends to resolve the problem.

"Society of the Blind Eye"

Mabel relizes that all of her summer romances have failed and wishes to forget them. Meanwhile, Dipper finds out that McGucket may be the author of the journals. When Dipper, Mabel, Wendy, and Soos ask McGucket if he's the author he caan't seem to remember. The crew's on a mission to find McGuckets memories and see if he really is the author.

"Blendin's Game"

Dipper and Mabel find out Soos hates his birthday. They try taking him to his favorite place, lazertag, but the twins end up being taken by Blendin and the time police. The only way back to Soos is to fight Blendin in the deadly Globnar.

"The Love God"

Dipper, Mabel, Wendy, and her friends are going to the Woodstock festival. At least untill Mabel finds Robbie unhappy and wants to help him find another girlfriend. After she made Robbie and Tambry fall in love by useing a love potion she stole from the Love God, everyone hated each other. Now Mabel and Dipper are trying to get Robbie and Tambry to break up to save their friend group.
