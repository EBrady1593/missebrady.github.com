History

The Turkey we know today was founded in 1923 after the Ottoman Empire(1299-1922) was defeated. After the sultan of the empire was defeted Turkey was declared a republic. The Ottoman Empire included Turkey, Greece, Bulgaria, Eygypt, Hungary, Macedonia,Romania, Jordan, Palestine, Lebanon, Syria, part of Arabia and a reasonable amount of the coastal strip of North Africa.

Before the Turkey we know now lot's of great and tragic things happeded:

  • In 1274 BCE, the Trojan war was fought in noethwest Turkey.

  • In 334 BCE, Alexander the Great conquers Anatolia

  • In 527 CE, the golden age of the Byzantium Empire starts.

  • In 1299 CE, The Ottoman Empire is founded.

  • In 1520 CE, Suleiman the Magnificent becomes the new ruler for the Ottoman Empire

  • In 1914 CE, the Ottoman Empire sides with Germany in World War I

  • In 1919 Ce, Turkey has a Turkish War for Independance

  • In 1929 CE, woman got their rights to vote and run for elected office.

Geography

Turkey is located in Asia and is surronded by Greece, Bulgaria Syrian Arab Republic, Iraq, Iran, Armenis, Azerbaijan and Georgia.

Climate: The climate of Turkey in the summer is hot and dry while the winter's are cool and wet. There's more snow than rainfall in Turkey.

Terrain: Turkey's terrain has rolling hills, central plateau's and high rugged mountains.
