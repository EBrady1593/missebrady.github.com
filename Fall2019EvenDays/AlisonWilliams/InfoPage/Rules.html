Rules

The following page contains information about the rules

Stay in foul territory

Stay in foul territory means that when you are on third and you take a lead and you are in foul territory and you get hit by a ball you are safe but it you are not in foul territory and you get hit by a ball you are out.

Leave when the pitcher released the ball

leave when the pitcher releases the ball means that if you are on base and you take a lead it can't be like baseball. When you play softball you have to wait until the pitcher almost releases the ball to take your lead.

Do not run into the ball when hit

Do not run into the ball when hit means if you are on base and a hard ground ball comes to you. You should not touch the ball because you will be out.

Respect

Respect means that when you are playing softball you should always be nice to you coaches, teammates, parents, other team and the umpires. The one thing that you should always respect is your equiment. Do not throw the bat, batting gloves, helmet and your glove.
