Positions

The following page contains information about the positions

Pitcher

The pitcher is the person that pitches the ball to the catcher. The pitcher is number 1 spot on the field.

Catcher

The catcher is the person that catches the ball from the pitcher. The catcher also gives signs to the pitcher. The catcher should throw the ball when there is a steal. The catcher is number 2 spot on the field.

First Base

The first baseman is the person that catches the ball from all the positions in the infield. The first baseman should catch the ball even if it is a bad throw. The first base is number 3 spot on the field.

Second Base

The second baseman is the person that is right next to second base and first base. The second baseman should field the ball and throw to first. If there is a runner on first and the second baseman gets a ground ball they can either tag the runner or throw the ball to the short stop and they should be at second waiting for the ball. The second baseman is number 4 on the field.

Third Base

The Third baseman is the person that is one third base and next to the short stop. The third baseman should field the ball and throw to first. If there is a runner on first and the third baseman gets a ground ball they should throw the ball to second and the second baseman should be at second waiting to get the ball. The third base is number 5 on the field.

Short Stop

The short stop is the person that is next to third base and right behind the pitcher. The short stop should field the ball and throw to first. If there is a runner on first and the short stop gets a ground ball they should throw to second base and the second baseman should be at second waiting for the ball. The short stop is number 6 on the field.

Left Field

The left fielder is the person that is in the grass behind third base. When there is a ball that gets through the infield or a linedrive or a popup the left fielder should throw to the cut which that is short stop. Left field is number 7 on the field.

Center Field

The center fielder is the person that is in the grass behind second base and short stop right in the center. When there is a ball that gets through the infield or a linedrive or a popup the center fielder should throw to the cut which that is short stop or second base, it matters were the ball is if it is on the left side short stop would go out, if it was on the right side the second baeman would fo out. Center field is number 8 on the field.

Right Field

The Right fielder is the person that is in the grass behind first base. When there is a ball that gets through the infield or a linedrive or a popup the right fielder should throw to the cut which that is second base. Right field is number 9 on the field.
