Softball bat

A softball bat can come in all sizes and colors. There are lots of brans of softball bats like an LXT or a PXT another one can be an Easton bat, there are lots of more bats that you can get. A softball bat can come end loaded which means that there is a weight on the end on the bat and that make the ball travel farther.

Softball glove

A softball glove can come in all sizes and colors. There are lots of brans of gloves like Rawlings, Wilsons and lots of more brans of gloves. A softball glove should not be too big or small because if it is too small or big you might have a tough time catching the ball.

Softball Cleats

Softball cleats can come in all sizes and colors. There are lots of brans of cleats like Mizuno, Under Armour, Nike and lots more brans of cleats. Softball cleats can come in metal cleats but you can only wear metal cleats in 14u or higher.

Batting Gloves

Batting gloves can come in all sizes and colors. There are lots of brans of batting gloves like Nike, Mizuno and lots more brans of batting gloves. Batting gloves can help prevent from getting blisters from you bat.

Softball Bag

A softball bag can come in all sizes and colors. There are lots of brans of softball bags like Mizuno, Nike and lots more of softball bags. A softball bag holds all of your equiment.

Catcher gear

Catcher gear can come in all sizes and colors. There are lots of brans of catcher gear like Mizuno and lots more brans of catcher gear. Catcher gear can help prevent from getting hit in the head by a ball, bat and more things that might happen.

Face Mask

Face Mask can come in all sizes and colors. There are lots of brans of face mask like rip it and lots more brans of face mask. Face mask can help prevent from getting hit in the face by a softball.

Tee

A tee can come in all sizes and colors. There are lots of brans of tee's like Mizuno and lots more brans of tee's. You need a tee if you want to hit softballs.
