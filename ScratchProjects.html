Miss Brady's Scratch Projects

The following projects are different project ideas that you can make. A few of the projects still have some bugs to work out.

Block Breaker (DX Ball):

Move your mouse to move the paddle Left and Right. Try to break all the blocks

Calculator:

Press the Calculator buttons to calculate values. It has all basic calculator functions.

Clicker Game with Upgrades:

Click on the cat. Clicking the buttons gives upgrades. There are multiple upgrades for each button.

Donkey Kong:

Left and Right arrows move Mario Left and Right. Up Arrow makes Mario climb ladder. Space key makes Mario Jump.

Dragon Fight (RPG):

There are 5 enemies you must defeat. Left Arrow - Charge. Right Arrow - Flee. Up Arrow - Sword. Down Arrow - Hide.

Monty Hall Problem:

Behind 1 of the doors there is a "Brand New Car". Behind the other
2 doors there are goats. Correctly chose where the car is.

TicTacToe:

Play Tic Tac Toe with a partner. Turns automatically switch. Be the first one to get 3 in a row.
