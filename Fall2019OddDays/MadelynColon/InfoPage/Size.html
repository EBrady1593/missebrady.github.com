Size

The following pages contain information about the sizes beagles can be. Even though, beagles can be different colors, they still fall in the same size classes.

13" Class

The 13" class is for beagles that stand 13" or shorter. The 13-inch variety is for hounds that don't exceed 13 inches in height at the shoulder. These beagles usually weigh about 18 pounds to 20 pounds, they may weigh more.

15" Class

The 15" class is for beagles that stand 13 1/16" or taller but 15" and shorter. If the beagle stands taller than 15" then it can't compete in the competitions. The 15-inch variety is for hounds that stand 13 inches to 15 inches at the shoulder. These beagles usually weigh about 20 pounds to 25 pounds, they may weigh more too.
