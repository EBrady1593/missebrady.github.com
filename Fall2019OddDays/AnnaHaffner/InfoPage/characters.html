Peppa Pig

Peppa is four years old. Her best friend is Suzy Sheep and she has a goldfish called Goldie. She wears a red dress, a white nappy and black shoes

George Pig

Peppa's younger brother. George is often seen in possession of his toy dinosaur, named "Mr. Dinosaur"

mummy pig

Mummy Pig is Peppa and George's mother. She is home employed and works on a computer. She also worked as a volunteer firefighter with the Mummies' Fire Service

daddy pig

Daddy Pig is Peppa and George's father. He works as an architectural engineer
