Small Forward

Small Forwards are typically smaller,faster, and leaner than power forwards and centers. But they are usually stronger and taller than the guards. The average height for a small forward is 6'7"-6'11. They can typically shoot the ball and drive to the basket.

Lebron James

Lebron James is a 6'9" small forward for the Los Angels Lakers. He is known as a GOAT(greates player of all time). He has played for the Cavaliers,heat, and now the lakers. He did not play college ball any were. He came right out of high school. He is a 3 time finals MVP. He is a 15 time all star. He has won many more awards.
