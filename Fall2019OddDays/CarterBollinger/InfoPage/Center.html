Center

The center is the tallest player on the court. They get the rebounds and set screens. They are usualy the strongest player on the court. They protect the paint and post players up. Sometimes centers can shoot the three which considers them a stretch five player.

Joel Embiid

Joel Embiid is a 7'0'' center for the philadelphia 76ers. He played college basketball with the Kansas Jayhawks. He was an all star in 2018 and 2019.He was on all nba second team in 2018 and 2019 and in those years he was on nba all defense second team.

Marc Gasol

Marc Gasol is a 6'11" center for the toronto raptors. He plays fiba basketball(olympics) for spain. That is his home country. Last season he won the finals with the raptors. He is a 3 time all start and was on the all nba first team.He was also on all nba 2nd team.

DeMarcus Cousins

DeMarcus Cousins is a 6'10" center for the Los Angeles Lakers. He played for the Kentucky Wildcats for one season in college. In his proffesional career, he played for the Kings, Pelicans, Warriors, and Lakers. He is a 4 time all star and on the all nba second team 2 times. He won 2 gold medals with the usa team and the spain team.
