Basketball

Basketball is a sport that is played world wide. The main objective is to get the ball through the basket. For points there are 3 pointers, field goals which are worth 2 points, and foul shots. Each foul shot is worth 1 point To score you can either shoot the ball or you can dunk it. You can also do layups of the backboard. The best spot to play professional basketball is in the united states. The league is called the NBA. Some of the best players in the world play in the NBA. In america, playing overseas is worse than the NBA. Usually you play overseas to get noticed by an NBA team after you come out of college. The reason why the NBA is better is because you get payed more.
