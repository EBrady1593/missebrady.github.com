Point Guard

Point guards,most of the time the shortest Player on the court. They are good shooters. They run the offense and spread the ball out to every player on their team.

Stephen Curry

Stephen Curry is 6'3" and is debatably the best shooter in NBA's History. Stephen Curry won MVP in 2015 and 2016 and was on the NBA all team for 2019,2018,2017,and many more years. He also won many more awards.

Ben Simmons

Ben Simmons is a 6'11" point guard for the 76ers. He is from Australia. He played 1 season in college for the LSU tigers. His dad played Professional Basketball too. Ben Simmons won rookie of the year in 2018 even though he got drafted in 2017. That is because he was hurt in 2017 which made his rookie season 2018.

Kyrie Irving

Kyrie Irving is a 6'2" Point Guard for the Brooklyn Nets. He won rookie of the year in 2011. He is a 6 time all star and was on all NBA third team in 2015. He was on the Cavaliers, Celtics, and Nets. He won mvp award for the 2015 all star game. He played college ball at Duke University.
