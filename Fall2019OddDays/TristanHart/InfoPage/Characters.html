Characters

The following page contains information about the characters from The Hunger Games trilogy.

Katniss Everdeen

Katniss is a highly skilled archer and at the beginning of the first book sixteen years old. She is well liked among most because her ability to provide prized game in District 12 where almost everyone has the Idea of starvation looming over them.

Peeta Mellark

Peeta is described as extemely likeable and a favorite within the Captiol and the Districts. He is was the Bakers son and specialized in decorating. He is sixteen at the start of the first Games. He rarly takes things to heart and gets upset.

Gale Hawthorne

Gale is Katniss' best friend and is eighteen when Katniss is drafted into the 74th Hunger Games. He is great with snares and can catch quite a lot of animals. He and Katniss are well known in District 13's black market.

Primrose Everdeen

Primrose, Prim, is Katniss' younger sister; her and Katniss are opposite in every way--even looks. Where as Katniss is a hunter Prim could not hurt a fly. She has talent for healing. She Has a special cat named Buttercup and a goat named lady. Both are disabled but she always cared for them.

Haymitch Abernathy

Haymitch is middle-aged alcholic and the victor of the 50th Hunger Games. He was Katniss and Peeta's mentor and guided them in the Hunger Games. He was able to get them sponsor therefore supplies which helped them greatly Tn the Hunger Games.

President Alma Coin

President Alma coin was the leader of District 13 and the rebellion against the Capitol. While President Snow is the main antagonist in the series Coin is revealed as a hidden villian in "Mockingjay." He demeanour is described as gray.

President Snow

President Coriolanus Snow is the ruler/dictator of Panem. He is the definition and evil and antagonist. It is said that to prolong his rule he used poision to kill people. He is constantly reminding the Districts that the Capitol is superior.
