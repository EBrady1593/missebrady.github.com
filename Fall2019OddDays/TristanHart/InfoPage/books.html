The Hunger Games

"The Hunger Games" centers around the 74th Hunger Games. The Book is from Katniss' point of view. It starts on the Reaping Day and is just simply centered around the Hunger Games no other hidden storylines.

Catching Fire

"Catching Fire" is focused more on the political aspects of the Hunger Games trilogy. There is a focus on the 75th Hunger Games as it is a Quarter Quell which makes it more significanat the most other Hunger Games. The true feelings of the Capitol and Districts begin to show.

Mockingjay

Finally, "MockingJay" is mostly all political. It surrounds Kattniss' journey fight against the Captitol. True destruction takes place and the entire series is concluded.
