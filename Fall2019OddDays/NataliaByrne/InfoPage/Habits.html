Their Habits

They Hunt and travel in packs. But the packs are 10 wolves per pack, not much in one pack. But sometimes wolves can be in packs of 30. Wolves may kill other wolves that are not apart of their pack. Wolves are also nocturnal and will hunt at night and sleep during the day.
