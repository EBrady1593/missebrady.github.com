This is going to be information about Wolves

Wolves are found in North America, Europe, Asia and North Africa. Male wolves are 66-180 Ibs while the female is 51-120 Ibs. Pack of wolves usually dont stay in one place. They travel as far as 12 miles to 20 kilometers a day.Wolves are very similar to dogs, they play, chew on bones, but when there threatened they growl , and snarl. Wolves do have friends, they howl to communicate with other memebers of there pack. They howl more to a pack then hang out with them.
