Wolves Diet

Wolves eat Meat that they have hunted. They can eat 20 Ibs of food during a Meal, for gray wolves. Red wolves eat smaller pray like rodents, insects and rabbits, they are not afraid to go outsdie the are also carnivorous diet and will also eat berries on occasions too. Gray wolves eat larger prey like moose, goats, sheep, and deer. A pack of wolves would try and find the weakest and sickest animal in a herd, circle it then kill it all together, then eat it.
