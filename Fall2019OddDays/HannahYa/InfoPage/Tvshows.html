Tv shows

The following page contains information about the Dc/Cw tv shows from 2012 to 2019.

The Arrow

The Arrow started in 2012. The Arrow is the first series in the Arrowverse. The Arrow is the longest lasting tv show that will have its final season during 2019-2020. There are currently 8 seasons and 162 total episodes

The Flash

The Flash started in 2014. The Flash is the second most series in the Arrowverse. The Flash also won the People's Choice Award for "Favorite New TV Drama" in 2014. There are currently 6 seasons and 117 total episodes.

Supergirl

Supergirl started out in 2015. Supergirl is the third series in the Arrowverse. There are currently 5 seasons and 90 total episodes.

Dc Legends

Dc Legends started in 2016. Dc Legends is the fourth series in the Arrowverse. There are currently 4 seasons with 67 total episodes.
