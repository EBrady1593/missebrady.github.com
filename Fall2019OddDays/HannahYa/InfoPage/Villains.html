The Villains

The folowing page contains information about the main villains of the tv shows.

The Arrow

Some villains include: Malcom Merlyn, China White, Deadshot, Edward Fyers, The Huntress, Frank Bertinelli, Firefly, Theodore Graynor, The Count, Cyrus Vanch, Billy Wintergreen, The Dodger, Deathstroke, Dollmaker, and Bronze Tiger

The Flash

Some villains include: King Shark, Wade Eiling, The Thinker, The Trickster, Killer Frost, Captain Cold, Gorilla Grodd, Savitar, Zoom, and Eobard Thawne

Supergirl

Some villains include: Lex Luthor, Reign, Red Daughter,Maxwell Lord, Astra IN-ZE, Morgan Edge, Rhea, Indigo, Agent Liberty, Non, and Manchester Black

Dc Legends

Some villains include: Vandal Savage, Legion of Doom(Eobard Thawne, Damien Darhk, Malcolm Merlyn, and Leonard Snart), Mallus, and Neron.
