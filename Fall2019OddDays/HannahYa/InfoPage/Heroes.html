The Heroes

The following page contains information about the main heroes of the tv shows.

The Arrow

Some heroes included are: The Arrow(Oliver Queen), John Diggle, Felicity Smoak, White Canary(Sarah Lance), Roy Harper, Thea Queen, Laurel Lance, and Quentin Lance.

The Flash

Some heroes included are: The Flash(Barry Allen), Killer Frost(Dr. Caitlin Snow), Vibe(Cisco Ramon) Iris West, Joe West, Elongated Man(Ralph Dibney), XS(Nora West-Allen), Kid Flash(Wally West), and H.R.

Supergirl

Some heroes included are: Supergirl(Kara Danvers), Alex Danvers, Martian Man Hunter(J'onn J'onzz), The DEO, Guardian(James Olsen), Winn Scott, Brainy, Dreamer(Nia Nal), and Mon-El.

Dc Legends

Some heroes included are: Rip Hunter, The Atom(Ray Palmer), White Canary(Sara Lance), Firestorm(Jefferson "Jax" Jackson, Martin Stein),Hawkgirl(Kendra Saunders), Hawkman(Carter Hall), Captain Cold(Leonard Snart), Heat Wave(Mick Rory), Vixen(Amaya Jiwe), The Time Bureau, Kuasa, Ava Sharpe, Zari Tomaz, Kid Flash, and John Constantine
