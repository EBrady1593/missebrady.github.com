The Arrow

The Cast: Stephen Amell stars as Oliver Queen, with principal cast members David Ramsey as John Diggle, Rick Gonzalez as Rene Ramirez, Juliana Harkavy as Dinah Drake and Katie Cassidy as Laurel Lance, and Emily Bett Rickards.

The Flash

The Cast:Grant Gustin stars as Barry, with principal cast members Candice Patton, Danielle Panabaker, Carlos Valdes, Tom Cavanagh, and Jesse L. Martin, Hartley Sawyer, Danielle Nicolet, and Jessica Parker Kennedy.

Supergirl

The Cast: Melissa Benoist stars as Kara, with principal cast members Mehcad Brooks, Chyler Leigh, David Harewood, and Katie McGrath, Jesse Rath, Sam Witwer, Nicole Maines, and April Parker Jones.

Dc Legends

The Cast:Principal cast members Brandon Routh, Caity Lotz, Maisie Richardson-Sellers, Tala Ashe, Amy Louise Pemberton, Nick Zano, and Dominic Purcell, Matt Ryan, Jes Macallan, and Courtney Ford.
