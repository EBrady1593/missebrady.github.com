Who Influences J-Fashion?

There are many people today who influence J-Fashion and its trends. With YouTube, Instagram, Twitter, and other social media platforms, people are able to discuss J-Fashion and share their favorite looks and brands with other people who are interested in similar styles. Here's some of the J-Fashion influencers of today!


Pixielocks/Jillian Vessey- Pixielocks is a YouTuber who posts videos about her lifestyle, sewing projects, and trips to Japan!
Baddiewinkle/Helen Elam - Baddiewinkle is a fashion influencer on Instagram. Many people love her outfits and her confidence in her style.

Kurebayashi - Kurebayashi is a popular fashion icon on Instagram. She dresses in lots of color, like decora.
