What is J-Fashion?

J-Fashion, otherwise known as Japanese Street Fashion, is simply the clothes people popularize by wearing out on the streets. J-Fashion originated in Japan. J-Fashion consists of many styles that exhibit clothes and accessories that aren't typically used in fashion. Dressing in J-Fashion gives people a way to stand out from the crowd and express emotions through colors, cute characters, and accessories! Many people who dress in J-Fashion say that the fashion helps create a community where they can bond with other people over clothes and styles.
