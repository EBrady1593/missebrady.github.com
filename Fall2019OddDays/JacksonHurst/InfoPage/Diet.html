What bearded dragons eat

Bearded dragons eat many types of insects, including dubia roaches, crickets, fly larva, and many types of worms.

Bearded dragons also eat a vairty of leafy greens like kale and vegtables like cucumbers and butternut squash.

Bearded dragons also eats a few types of fruit like blue berries and cantaloupe. Look up if something is okay to feed your bearded dragon before you feed them.
