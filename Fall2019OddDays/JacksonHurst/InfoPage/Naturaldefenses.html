Bearded Dragon defenses

Bearded dragon need to have natural defenses to defend themselves, one of these defenses is spikes. Bearded dragons are covered in spikes that grip and can cause some damage, especially when they flail.

Bearded dragons have sharp teeth and are not afraid to use them if they feel threatened. They also have a venom that can't harm humans, but is effective on smaller animals.

Bearded dragons also have another defense. They can puff up their beard and turn it black to intimadate predators.
