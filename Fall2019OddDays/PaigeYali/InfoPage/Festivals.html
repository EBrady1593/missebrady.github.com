Ephrata

The Ephrata's most know festival is the Ephrata Fair. Which takes place in spring. We have been doing the fair for 101 years. It was created in 1919. Origanly it lasted one day. But now it lasts 5 days.

A joint like festival with the fair is the Ephrata Farmer fair. It started in 1920 and takes place at the park. It takes place at the same time as the fair.

Eberbach

Eberbach has a Rheingau music festival. It takes place in the summer. It was started in 1987. It is a very popular festival it is a international festival. There are multipule types of music. The most common type of music there is classical.

    • Another festival is the WinterZauber festival. It happends in late November. It takes place at the St. Nicholas market. People create crafts and baked goods. There are christmas decorations through out the festival. It is a one day festival.
