In this project I am comparing Ephrata Pa to its sister city of Eberbach Germany

Ephrata as we know is a mostly flat land with some mountains and hills around the area. Ephrata is in Lancaster County. Eberbach is mostly flat. Its elevation is only 440 feet above sea level. It is in the state of Baden-Wurttemburg in Germany.
