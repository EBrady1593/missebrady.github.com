Best Rated Chinese Restaurants

Chinese restaurants are tested and tasted to find out the best of the best. In the article I used, the Chinese Magic Noodle House and the Beijing Pie House are rated the best.

Chinese Magic Noodle House, Chandler

The Chinese Magic Noodle House is given a 4.3 star rating. eventhough it's very delicous. They have soups and dishes made from hand pulled noodles at this well lit Chinese eatery.

Beijing Pie House, Monterey Park

The Beijing Pie House has a 4.2 star rating. They specialize in northern Chinese fare, including noodles, stuffed buns, and pancakes.
