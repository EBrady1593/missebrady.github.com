Types of Chinese Food

Chinese food comes in many shapes and sizes. Different tastes are involved, as well. Maybe you want sweet or maybe something sour. Or even both.

Eggrolls

Eggrolls are deep fried appetizers found in Chinese Restaurants in the US. They contain cabbage, pork, and other fillings wrapped in wheat flower skin. The wheat flower skin is fried as well.

Ramen

Ramen translates to pulled noodles. The noodles are chinese wheat noodles, with fish or meat broth. The dishes are usually flavored with different sauces.
