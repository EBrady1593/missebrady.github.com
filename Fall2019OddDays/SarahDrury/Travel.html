History

South Korea officially began its establishment in 1948. South Korea had been under the rule of Japan, but they had surrendered. Korea broke into two different parts known as South Korea and North Korea. North Korea is strictly democratic and South Korea is neither. You're free in South Korea to believe in whatever you like.

Geography

The geography of South Korea is fairly mountainous. Valleys and coastal plains are common. South Korea is surrounded by mostly water. The coastline is around all three seas.

Cities

Cities in Korea are Busan, Jeju-si, Changwon, and Ulsan.
